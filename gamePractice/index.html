Play Instructions

The goal of the game is to have the highest score possible

Eat food bowls to increase your score! The more poits you get, the hardest it will be! You can chase the food by moving up, down, left and right using the arrow keys of your keyboard. But be careful, there will be enemies coming towards you, too

You will start the game with 9 lives. Each time you miss a food bowl or you touch an enemy you'll lose a life. Once you run out of lives, it'll be game over.
