Play Instructions

Goal

The goal of the game is to reach the highest possible score by feeding your cat. You can do this by reaching for the food bowls on the screen.

How to play

Avoid contact with any dogs you see. Every time a dog touches you, you will lose one of your nine cat lives. When you see mice you do not need to worry, as mice are harmless to you. However, if you do not tell them to go away they will stay with you, making it more difficult to see where your food and enemies are coming from!

Use the arrow keys to move your cat up, down, left or right. Press the spacebar to shoo mice them away

Have fun!
