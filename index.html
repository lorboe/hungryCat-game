Play Instructions

The goal of the game is to reach the highest possible score by feeding your cat.

Eat food bowls to increase your score! The more poits you get, the hardest it will be! But be careful, there will be enemies coming towards you, too

You can use the arrow keys in your keyboard to move the cat across the screen. Make sure to chase the food bowls, as this is how you will get your points. However, be careful not to touch any dog or else you'll lose one of your nine lives!

Finally, you will also see mice on the screen. Mice are harmless, but they will not go away until you tell them to. Press the spacebar of your keyboard to shoo them away

Have fun!
